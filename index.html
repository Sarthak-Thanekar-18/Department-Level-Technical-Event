About the Event

The Department of Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning proudly presents AIML TechFest 2025 - our inaugural technical event designed to bring together bright minds from across engineering disciplines.

This is the first time our department is organizing a tech event of this magnitude, featuring competitions, workshops, and showcases that celebrate innovation in AI, ML, and emerging technologies.

Open to students from Engineering, IT, AIML, Computer Science, and Diploma programs, this event offers a unique platform to demonstrate your technical prowess, learn from industry experts, and network with fellow tech enthusiasts.

Rules & Guidelines

Eligibility

  • • Open to all undergraduate students from Engineering, IT, AIML, CS, Diploma & Degree programs
  • • Valid college ID card required for entry
  • • Students from all colleges are welcome
  • • Registration deadline: 3 days before event

Team Participation

  • • Technical Quiz: Individual participation only
  • • Coding Challenge: Individual or teams of max 2
  • • Project Showcase: Teams of max 4 members
  • • Paper Presentation: Individual or teams of max 3

General Rules

  • • Participants must register separately for each event
  • • Multiple event participation is allowed
  • • Bring your own laptops for coding events
  • • Punctuality is mandatory; late entries not allowed

Code of Conduct

  • • Maintain professional behavior throughout the event
  • • No plagiarism or unfair means allowed
  • • Judges' decision will be final and binding
  • • Organizers reserve the right to disqualify rule violators
