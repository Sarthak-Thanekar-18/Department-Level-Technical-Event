About the Technical Event

AIML Tech Event 2025 is a campus-wide technical event open to students from all departments. It focuses on coding challenges, logical thinking, and practical problem-solving. The event encourages innovation and cross-department participation.

Participants engage in beginner-to-advanced level coding competitions. Real-world problem statements help build industry-relevant skills. Certificates and recognition are awarded to all participants.

Rules & Guidelines

Eligibility Criteria

  • Open to undergraduate and postgraduate students from all engineering disciplines
  • Valid student ID card is mandatory for participation
  • Students can participate individually or in teams (as per event requirements)

Participation Rules

  • Participants must adhere to the event schedule and reporting times
  • Original work is mandatory; plagiarism will lead to disqualification
  • Decision of judges and organizing committee is final
  • Participants must maintain professional conduct throughout the event
  • Registration confirmations will be sent via email after successful submission
